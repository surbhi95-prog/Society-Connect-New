Workflow

How It Works

From society onboarding to daily management — our streamlined platform handles it all in three powerful steps.

01

Society Onboards

The managing committee contacts our team to get a dedicated society instance. We configure the complete structure including wings, flat numbers, and admin access.

  • Custom wing & flat configuration
  • Super Admin credentials handed over
  • Setup completed within 24 hours
02

Residents Register

Committee shares the portal link. Residents self-register by selecting their wing and flat number. The admin approves accounts to ensure only verified residents gain access.

  • Self-registration with flat mapping
  • Admin approval workflow
  • Secure, unique resident profiles
03

Go Live & Manage

Your society is live! Admins can post notices, configure maintenance schedules, track complaints, and manage resident data — all from a powerful, intuitive dashboard.

  • Real-time notice broadcasting
  • Online maintenance payments
  • Full complaint management
